{% blocktrans %} I do an initial evaluation of my students which includes:

  1. A Par-Q questionnaire
  2. Analysing the current physical condition and body composition
  3. Analysing the current diet and stress levels
  4. Setting a end-goal and an ideal training plan in the time you have

I aim to help my students reach their goals by utilizing safe and efficient training methods suited for each case.

It's not about the training!

It's about the ideal training plan for you!

Everyone is different, so in order for you to get results every training plan must be personalized.

I support the change to a healthier lifestyle where you feel supported and safe!
